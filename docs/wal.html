Joining a Weave Group

You have clicked on a link to join a Weave group in Moss, but currently "deep-links" aren't enabled.

But don't worry! To join the group, you can just click on Join Group in the Moss app itself, and then paste in the URL that brought you to this page.

If you haven't installed Moss yet, you can download it here!
